FAQ

Is my first day really free?

Yes! Your first day at Spark is completely free. Just register online or come to any location and let our team know you’re new.

Do I need to book a desk in advance?

For Open Space, you can just walk in and pick any available spot. However, Fixed Desks and Meeting Rooms must be reserved ahead of time through our website to ensure availability.

Are meeting rooms included in my plan?

Meeting rooms are not included in standard memberships and are reserved separately. You can easily book one whenever you need it.

Can I bring guests or have calls?

Of course. Guests are welcome for short visits, and phone or video calls are perfectly okay in common areas or dedicated call zones.

Can I bring my pet to Spark?

We love furry coworkers! Spark is pet-friendly, provided your pet is well-behaved and doesn't disturb other members.
